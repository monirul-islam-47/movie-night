What happens next

From download to movie night

  1. Install or open. Launch Movie Night on the host desktop.
  2. Choose a movie. Preparation progress stays visible and the room opens as soon as it is watchable.
  3. Invite your guest. Send the guest link from the room; keep the private host link to yourself.
  4. End safely. Goodnight closes the server, preparation, and connection helpers together.
Advanced: Docker, source, and portable alternatives

Docker and source remain supported for technical hosts and as the temporary macOS fallback. The Linux portable tar is available beside the AppImage.
